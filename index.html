About BlindWrite

BlindWrite is an innovative assistive technology startup that bridges the gap between neural activity and communication. We convert brain EEG signals into readable Braille output, empowering visually impaired individuals with a new level of independence. Our mission is to provide a wearable, adaptive, and intelligent solution that translates thoughts into tactile language. From real-time signal decoding to personalized Braille feedback, BlindWrite is redefining accessibility.
